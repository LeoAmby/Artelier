About

Leo is an institute created primarily to bring individuals who have passion in art and ensure they reach out to the relevant audiences. Art is life and it enables individuals to associate and bring the best of who they really are. Considering that there are various scopes of art, Leo facilitates the need for artists to express themselve in whatever genres that suits them and those they are strong at. Art has not only been a tool of communication, but has also ensured that artist handle various societal issues. It brings relevance when one is in a position to elaborate themselve clearly in relation to the issues at hand and the audience in question.

The forms of art which are featured most under Leo include:

  1. ✰ Photography
  2. ✰ Writing and Publishing
  3. ✰ Drawing and Painting
  4. ✰ Fashion Design
  5. ✰ Digital Design
